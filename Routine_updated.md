# Core Values & Philosophy

## Vision
 #### To feel deeply, to love fiercely, to learn relentlessly, to create something that outlasts you, and to leave the world (or at least one small corner of it) a little better than you found it

## Life Pillars
- ***Salat*** (spiritual grounding)
- ***Quran*** (daily practice)
- ***Actionable Knowledge*** (learning with purpose)
- **Security Research** (domain expertise)
- **Physical Health** (eating, drinking, exercise, sleep)

## Core Philosophy
The Quran daily, sleep for 7+ hours, eat & drink halal and the best foods available, exercise regularly, keep yourself clean and pure, and most importantly: **Study and work recklessly**.

## Elite Education Commitment

# Practical Routine

## Time Allocation
| Sphere | Period | Duration |
| :---: | --------------- | :----------: |
| Soul  | WakeUp → Fazar  |      1 hour       |
| Mind  | Fazar → Magrib  |      12 hours     |
| Body  | Magrib → Sleep  |      10 hours     |

## Daily Execution

### Soul (1 hour) - Early Morning [WakeUp to Fazar]
- Tahajjud (night prayer)
- Quran recitation & study
- Spiritual preparation for the day

### Mind (12 hours) - Peak Hours [Fazar to Magrib]
- Reading & research books
- Security research & bug analysis
- Actionable knowledge building
- Daily tasks & responsibilities
- Deep work sessions on: AI, Cryptography, and programming (Rust/Python/Go)
- *Note: Break into focused 90-min sprints with short breaks*

### Body (10 hours) - Evening/Night [Magrib to Sleep]
- Sleep: 7+ hours (minimum)
- Optional nap: 1+ hours (if needed, after lunch)
- Exercise: 2 hours
- Meals: Halal, nutritious food
- Hygiene & self-care
- Relaxation/recovery
